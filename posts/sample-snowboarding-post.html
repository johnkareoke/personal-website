Amazing Snowboarding Adventure

January 15, 2024

This was an incredible snowboarding trip to the backcountry. The conditions were perfect with fresh powder and clear skies.

The Journey

We started early in the morning, hiking up to the ridge with our splitboards. The snow was perfect - light and fluffy, exactly what you want for backcountry riding.

Technical Details

  • Location: Backcountry near Mount Baker
  • Conditions: Fresh powder, 12 inches
  • Temperature: 25°F (-4°C)
  • Equipment: Splitboard setup

The descent was absolutely epic. We found some amazing lines through the trees and open bowls. The snowboarding was challenging but incredibly rewarding.

Photography

I managed to capture some great shots during the trip. The combination of snowboarding and photography made this an unforgettable experience.

This trip really highlighted why I love both snowboarding and photography - the combination of adrenaline and artistic expression is unbeatable.
