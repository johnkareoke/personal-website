Epic Cycling Tour Through Europe

June 20, 2024

This summer I embarked on an incredible cycling adventure across Europe. Starting from Amsterdam, I cycled through Germany, Austria, and into Italy.

The Route

The journey covered over 2,000 kilometers through some of the most beautiful cycling routes in Europe. The EuroVelo network provided excellent infrastructure for long-distance cycling.

Highlights

  • Amsterdam to Cologne: Flat, scenic routes along rivers
  • Through the Alps: Challenging climbs with breathtaking views
  • Italian Lakes: Beautiful coastal cycling
  • Final Destination: Venice

The cycling was both challenging and rewarding. The physical demands of long-distance cycling were balanced by the incredible scenery and cultural experiences.

Photography Along the Way

I captured hundreds of photos during the trip. The combination of cycling and photography allowed me to document the journey in a unique way.

This cycling adventure was one of the most memorable experiences of my life. The freedom of traveling by bike, the physical challenge, and the cultural immersion made it truly special.
