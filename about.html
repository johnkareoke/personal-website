About

A bit about me and what I do

Hello! I'm John Carolin, and this is my personal space on the web where I share my adventures, thoughts, and creative projects. I'm passionate about photography, outdoor activities, and exploring the world around me.

What I Do

I spend my time cycling through beautiful landscapes, snowboarding in the mountains, and capturing moments through photography. When I'm not outdoors, I work on software projects and share my experiences through writing and images.

My Interests

Photography

Capturing moments and landscapes through the lens, from urban scenes to natural wonders.

Cycle Touring

Exploring the world on two wheels, discovering new places and meeting interesting people along the way.

Snowboarding

Riding the slopes and backcountry, experiencing the thrill of winter sports in beautiful mountain settings.

Software Development

Creating useful tools and applications, including the Panoplugin project for panoramic photography.

My Story

I've been fortunate to travel to many amazing places and experience different cultures. From the mountains of New Zealand to the coastlines of Europe, each journey has taught me something new and provided inspiration for my photography and writing.

This website serves as a repository of those experiences - a collection of images, stories, and projects that I hope will inspire others to explore and create.

Get in Touch

I'm always interested in connecting with fellow travelers, photographers, and outdoor enthusiasts. Feel free to reach out!
